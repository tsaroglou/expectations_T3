Random group allocation

For the rest of the experiment, the participants in this session will be randomly split into two groups. When indicated, your decisions will affect the payments of the other group.

For example, in the next page you will be asked to vote on a tax rate. The tax rate that you will vote on will not affect your payment or the payment of anyone else in your group. You decisions will only affect the payments received by the members of the other group.

Neither the members of your nor the other group will find out about your decisions. You do not need any additional information regarding your groupmates or the individuals in the other group because any further interaction will not be required.
