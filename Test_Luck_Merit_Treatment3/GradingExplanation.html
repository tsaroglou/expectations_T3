Point System

In this test, instead of counting correct answers, we will be counting points.

Each question of the test can either give you 0 or 1 point.

Each time you answer a question incorrectly or do not respond to it, you might still receive a point for the question with a 15% probability.

Each time you answer a question correctly you receive the point with a 70% probability. But, despite giving the correct answer, you might still get 0 points for the question with a 30% probability.

Therefore you might receive a point even if you are incorrect in a question (with a 15% probability) but you might receive no points for a question even if you are correct (with a 30% probability).

For simplicity, from now on we are going to be calling the score you receive as a result of the above mechanism the "point score" and your total correct answers simply as "real total correct answers".
