How the game is selected in each round

Before each round begins, you and your partner will each vote for which game you would prefer to play in that round.

  • If both participants vote for Game 2, then Game 2 will be played.
  • If either participant votes for Game 1, then Game 1 will be played.

In other words, Game 2 is only selected if both you and your paired participant agree to play it in a given round.

After voting, both you and your partner will learn each other's votes and which game will be played.

Making your choice

After the game is selected, you and your partner will each independently and simultaneously choose between Option A and Option B for the selected game.

Finally, you and your paired participant learn about each other's choices and the resulting points each has earned. Then, you move on to the next round and the process is repeated.
