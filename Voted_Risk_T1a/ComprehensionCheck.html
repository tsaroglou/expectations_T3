Welcome! In this experiment, you will be randomly paired with another participant. You and your partner will play a series of at least {{ C.min_rounds }} rounds of simple games. Your choices and your partner’s choices determine how many points each of you earns each round. At the end, your total points are converted into a real‐money bonus. The conversion rate is the following:

25 Points = 1 Euro

What is a “game”?

A game is the table that shows how many points each player receives, depending on each player's actions. There are two games, Game 1 and Game 2. Before each round, you will vote on which game you prefer to play.

How to read a game table

Each table shows your two choices (Action C or Action D) on the left, and your partner’s two choices across the top. In each cell, the first number is the points you earn, and the second number is the points your partner earns.

Summary

    In each round, the sequence of events is the following:

    1. Voting: You and your partner each vote for Game 1 or Game 2.

    2. Game selection: If both of you vote for Game 2, then Game 2 is played; otherwise, Game 1 is played.

    3. Action choice: You choose Action A or Action B for the selected game.

    4. Points: You and your partner earn the points shown in the point table cell corresponding to your choices. These points are added to your running total points.
