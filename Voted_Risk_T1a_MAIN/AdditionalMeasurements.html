In the following table there are 4 rows corresponding to Decision 1, Decision 2, Decision 3, and Decision 4. In each decision, you must choose between two options: Option Left (L) and Option Right (R). In each decision, the chosen option will specify an earning for you and an earning for another participant in the session. All participants in the session will take the same four decisions.

Once all the participants in the session have taken their decisions, one of the participants will be randomly picked. For the participant that is picked, one of their four decisions will be randomly selected and whatever option they chose in that decision the payments will be made.

Thus, the selected participant will receive the amount of Pounds that, according to their option choice in the selected decision, corresponds to them. Additionally, another participant in the session will be randomly selected and will receive the amount of Pounds that corresponds to “the other participant”.

These additional payments will be made after the conlusion of the experiment.
