Instructions (Part 1)

In this experiment, you will interact with another participant. You will earn points based on the choices you and that other participant make.

At the end of the experiment, your total points will be converted into a real-money bonus according to the following conversion formula:

25 Points = 1 Euro

The points you accumulate will be summed over all rounds.

Understanding the game

We represent a game using a table. In each round, you will choose a row of that table (Option A or Option B) and your paired participant will choose a column (also Option A or Option B).
